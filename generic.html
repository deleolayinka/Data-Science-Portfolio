About Me

I am a smart, creative, hard-working, and enthusiastic individual. I believe in teamwork and expert information-sourcing & analysis as the means for achieving positive results in any organization. I have the ability to handle complex assignments effectively and make relevant decisions independently when necessary.

I firmly believe in the fundamental importance of trust, openness, teamwork, professionalism, and love for the job I do. I am an ambitious and self-motivated individual with a keen ability to work in an establishment where my academic, technical, administrative, and organizational skills will be optimally utilized.

I have acquired three years and nine months of experience working out of the Enterprise Intelligence unit at the most respected payments systems central switch in Africa, Nigeria Inter-Bank Settlement Systems (NIBSS) PLC, as a Business Data Analyst. I am responsible for developing hypotheses based on the client's business needs and using available data to confirm or reject such hypotheses, I employ the use of raw unprocessed data from the NIBSS databases and warehouse to derive novel insights to improve existing processes, reduce costs, develop new products, and drive critical decision making.

In the last three years, I have spent a lot of time and effort in improving my data visualization & presentation skills and growing my personal network of experienced data scientists. Using the Microsoft Power BI data visualization tool, I recently developed and deployed a dashboard at NIBSS that explores possible indications of client attrition, leverage predictive analytics to predict future attrition, and identify major drivers in a bid to propose possible client retention strategies and prevent at-risk clients from leaving.

My current goal is to obtain an advanced data science degree and improve my insights presentation/data storytelling with the experience garnered at NIBSS PLC. I am interested in teamwork and therefore participate in organizing team bonding events and supporting my colleagues. I feel these activities will make it possible for me to make a difference and gain the required leadership skills necessary to attain set goals.
